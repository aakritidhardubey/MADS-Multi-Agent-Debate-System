About Help

Help & Guide

🚀 Getting Started

Step 1: Click "Start Your First Debate" on the welcome screen

Step 2: (Optional) Toggle "Live Debate Mode" ON to watch real-time streaming

Step 3: Enter your debate topic or use voice input (microphone button)

Step 4: Choose an AI model based on your needs

Step 5: Click "Launch Debate" and watch the analysis

🤖 Choosing the Right Model

  • Llama 3.1 8B Instant: Fast responses, great for quick debates and general topics
  • Llama 3.3 70B Versatile: Most powerful, best for complex topics requiring deep reasoning

🎥 Live Debate Mode

What it does: Watch AI agents debate in real-time with typing animations

How to use: Toggle "Live Debate Mode" ON before starting your debate

Features: Live timer, status updates, character-by-character text streaming

Stop button: Click "Stop" anytime to see complete results immediately

🎙️ Voice Features

Speech-to-Text: Click microphone button to dictate your topic or questions

Text-to-Speech: Click speaker icons to hear arguments read aloud

Speed Control: Click sliders icon (⚙️) to adjust speech speed and pitch

💡 Tips for Best Results

  • Be Specific: Clear, focused topics get better analysis
  • Frame as Questions: "Should X?" or "Is Y better than Z?" work well
  • Avoid Vague Topics: Instead of "Technology", try "Should AI replace human customer service?"
  • Use Live Mode: More engaging experience with real-time streaming
  • Use Follow-ups: Ask clarifying questions to explore specific points deeper
  • Check Analytics: View visualizations to understand argument strength

📊 Understanding Analytics

Argument Strength: Shows which side won based on judge's verdict

Key Topics: Color-coded word cloud (green=FOR, red=AGAINST, blue=both)

Side Comparison: Multi-dimensional radar chart comparing both sides

💬 Using Follow-up Questions

After the debate completes, you can ask unlimited follow-up questions:

  • "Which side had stronger evidence?"
  • "Can you explain point 3 from the arguments against?"
  • "What are the real-world implications?"
  • "Are there any compromises between both positions?"

Tip: Use voice input (microphone button) for hands-free questions!

📄 Exporting Results

PDF Report: Professional formatted document with all arguments and conclusion

Text File: Plain text version for easy sharing

Copy to Clipboard: Quick copy for pasting into documents or emails

🌍 Multi-Language Support

Click the language selector to choose from 10+ languages. Speech recognition will automatically adapt to your selected language.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does a debate take?

A: Typically 30-60 seconds depending on the model and topic complexity.

Q: Can I debate any topic?

A: Yes! From technology to philosophy, politics to lifestyle choices.

Q: Are the arguments biased?

A: Our agents are designed to present balanced perspectives. The "For" agent supports, the "Against" agent challenges, and the Judge provides neutral analysis.

Q: Can I save my debates?

A: Yes! Use the export options to save as PDF or text file.

About AI Debate Arena

Multi-Agent Debate System

AI Debate Arena is an advanced multi-agent collaborative system that analyzes complex topics from multiple perspectives. Our intelligent agents work together to provide you with comprehensive, balanced insights.

How It Works

🤖 Three Specialized AI Agents:

  • Agent For: Presents 4 well-researched arguments supporting your topic with evidence and examples
  • Agent Against: Provides 4 analytical counterarguments highlighting potential flaws and risks
  • Final Judge: Delivers a clear verdict declaring which side wins with detailed reasoning

🎥 Live Debate Mode:

Watch AI agents debate in real-time with typing animations! Toggle Live Mode to see arguments unfold character-by-character with live status updates and timer.

💬 Interactive Follow-up System:

After the debate, ask unlimited follow-up questions to dive deeper into specific arguments, clarify points, or explore implications. Our specialized follow-up agent maintains full context of the debate.

📊 Real-time Analytics:

Automatic visualization of debate results with interactive charts showing argument strength based on judge's verdict, color-coded word clouds, and multi-dimensional comparisons.

🎯 Key Features:

  • Live streaming debate mode with typing animations
  • Clear winner declaration in every debate
  • Speech-to-text input (voice dictation)
  • Text-to-speech output (read aloud) with adjustable speed
  • Multi-language support (10+ languages)
  • Interactive visualizations and analytics
  • Export results as PDF or text files
  • Multiple AI models to choose from
