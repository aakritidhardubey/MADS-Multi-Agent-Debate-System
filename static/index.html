Help & Guide

🚀 Getting Started

Click Start a Debate, fill in your topic, pick a model, customise the settings, then hit Launch Debate.

⚙️ Customisation

  • Depth: Brief (short), Standard (balanced), Deep (thorough with evidence)
  • Args per Side: 2–6 arguments each side
  • Tone: Balanced · Aggressive · Academic · Casual · Socratic
  • Focus: General · Economic · Ethical · Scientific · Social
  • Live Mode: Watch arguments appear in real-time

💬 Follow-up Q&A

Ask unlimited follow-up questions in the chat after the debate finishes.

📜 History

All past debates are saved. Click History in the top bar to browse them.
